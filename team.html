Our Mission

We make impactful technology.

CFI is a social tech organization committed to the work of NGOs across the world, an international developlment agency centered around technology in the developing world, a social enterprise dedicated to helping those who need it most. Put this together, and we drive impact.



Core Values

We shift, we grow, we stumble, we pivot and we continually refine our work and scope—yet amidst the ever-moving landscape of international development, there are core values that act as constants for Splash.

People

People first. People second. People third.


Quality

Beautiful products that function and last.


Honesty

No drama, no surprises, no BS.


Joy

Kids at heart, seeing potential everywhere.


Mistakes

Make them, don’t repeat them.
