How Do We Create Impact?

Coding for Impact works with foreign nonprofits & NGOs and builds technology that gives opportunities to developing communities. All organizations have the pieces in place to widen their reach. We simply need to create sustainable systems for them to create a more efficient, effective, and equitable impact.



Tech & Inequality

We differ from international development organizations in recognizing that the infrastructure is already there: it is our inability to see the intersections between economic situations and innovative technology that pushes our society back. Our unique approach brings technology into socioeconomic work for a sustainable solution that is locally appropriate.

Our Approach


Cultivating Global Partnerships

Creating change worldwide

Coding for Impact takes what works for big-tech firms — their ability to invest in supplemental technology that is impactful — and brings it to the nonprofit sector across the world to allow them to drive a greater difference. There is no reason why underfunded charities cannot find volunteers, why autoimmune disorders cannot be found online. We change that in our partnerships. With a diverse set of partners extending from Los Angeles to Cambodia, from Austria to Uganda, we have a global impact.
