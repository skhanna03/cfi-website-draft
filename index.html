Our Philosophy

Big Ambitions, Tangible Change

We empower nonprofits to create sustainable change with technological solutions.

Our group of thinkers and leaders develop technology that drives an impact in our society. We create independent projects that solve some of the most relevant issues our society faces. We partner with leading nonprofits across the globe to build applications and create infrastructure that makes nonprofit work accessible in developing nations. By creating scalable technology and sustainable organizations, we're able to set a bold goal of every developing world nonprofit delivering change with us: making sanitation accessible to rural children in Cambodia; higher-education in Uganda; eradicating domestic servitude for women in Haiti; and more.
